Ashapuri Refrigeration, founded in 2012 by Mr. Nandkishor Kotkar, is a distinguished engineering company with a strong focus on refrigeration and air conditioning. Mr. Kotkar, a technocrat with over 30 years of remarkable experience, has guided the company to substantial growth in business volumes and customer base. This success is attributed to the goodwill and trust earned from clients over the years. In 2014, Ashapuri Refrigeration strategically acquired M/s Padmaja Enterprises, a well-established company with a 20-year legacy in serving the food processing sector.

This acquisition has significantly enhanced Ashapuri Refrigeration's capabilities and market reach, allowing it to offer cutting-edge solutions and unparalleled service to a broader clientele. Specializing in ammonia refrigeration, multipurpose cold storage, industrial and dairy refrigeration, pre-cooling ripening chambers, and solutions for food processing and fisheries, Ashapuri Refrigeration is committed to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, continually striving to exceed expectations and deliver excellence.

Quality focus is part of our core values and is synonymous with excellence, value to customers, conformance to specifications and standards, fitness for use, and ultimately, customer satisfaction.

Refrigeration Industry Overview

The refrigeration industry is pivotal for various sectors, offering essential cooling solutions. It encompasses innovations in ammonia refrigeration, multipurpose cold storage, industrial and dairy refrigeration, pre-cooling ripening chambers, and food processing and fisheries applications.

Ashapuri Refrigeration Services, founded in 2012 by Mr. Nandkishor Kotkar, is a premier engineering company specializing in refrigeration and air conditioning solutions. With over 30 years of industry experience, we have grown significantly, earning a strong reputation for delivering high-quality services. Our expertise lies in the design, execution, and maintenance of a wide range of refrigeration projects, including cold storages, blast freezers, IQF systems, plate freezers, and brine chillers. We also design and manufacture industrial cooling equipment, such as process chillers and condensing units.

Our services extend to the sourcing, installation, testing, commissioning, and maintenance of refrigeration plants, making us a one-stop solution for all industrial refrigeration needs. We are committed to providing cost-effective, customized solutions that prioritize energy efficiency and environmental sustainability. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering innovative applications while maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality.

As an exclusive service provider for Danfoss India Ltd. in the West Zone and an authorized integrator for Alfa Laval India Pvt. Ltd., we are proud to be at the forefront of the refrigeration industry. Our vision is to continue growing by introducing new technologies and expanding our manufacturing capabilities, ensuring we meet the evolving needs of our clients.
